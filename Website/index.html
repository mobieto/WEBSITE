JOHN W. BOOTH

My name is John Wilkes Booth. I am an artist residing in England, Peterborough. My work consists of abstract and cubist art styles, which I combine with unusual drawing media - such as tea and coffee, burnt tree branches, and sometimes even my own blood. On this website you'll find my artworks along with a shop where you are able to purchase my very own art. Feel free to contact me here too if you would like to commission me or have any questions.

Thank you for visiting!
